Welcome to EHPSA

Welcome to the official website of the Eastern Highlands Province Student Association (EHPSA) at Divine Word University. We are a vibrant community of students dedicated to preserving our rich cultural heritage, supporting academic excellence, and fostering unity among students from Eastern Highlands Province.
