Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join EHPSA?

Any student from Eastern Highlands Province at DWU. Alumni and supporters are also welcome.

How do I pay my membership fee?

Please reach out to the executives or the treasurer in charge for payment instructions.

Can I join at any time?

Yes! Membership is open throughout the academic year.

What is the Alumni & Supporters membership?

Open to alumni and others who wish to support the human resource development of Eastern Highlands Province.
