About Eastern Highlands Province Student Association

Uniting the sons and daughters of the Land of the Rising Sun

Who We Are

The Eastern Highlands Province Student Association (EHPSA) is a vibrant and dynamic organization representing students from Eastern Highlands Province studying at Divine Word University (DWU), Madang Campus. Established to foster unity, preserve cultural heritage, and promote academic excellence, EHPSA serves as a home away from home for our members.

With a rich history rooted in the traditions of the "Land of the Rising Sun," our association brings together students from all districts — from Goroka to Kainantu, from Henganofi to Lufa, and from Unggai-Bena to Daulo and Okapa. We celebrate the diversity within our province while standing united under one banner.

EHPSA is more than just a student association — it is a family. We support each other academically, socially, and spiritually, ensuring that every member feels valued, heard, and empowered to achieve their full potential.

Our History

The Eastern Highlands Province Student Association was founded by a group of visionary students who recognized the need for a unified body to support students from the province at Divine Word University. What began as small gatherings of Eastern Highlands students has grown into one of the most vibrant and active provincial student associations on campus.

We honor and acknowledge the pioneer initiators who laid the foundation of EHPSA — their vision and dedication continue to inspire us today.
